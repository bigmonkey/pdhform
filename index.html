Payday Loans

Payday loans are designed for short-term, emergency funding. These loans are fast, convenient and do not require good credit so can be an expensive form of credit. Please review your terms carefully before accepting a loan.

Rates and Fees

APR and fees vary and are disclosed by your lender once your are approved. Example loan costs from our network of lenders might be a 14 day loan with a fee of $12 per $100 borrowed and an APR of 312.86%.

Non-payment and Collections

Non-payment and collection policies depend on your the lender and should be disclosed in your loan agreement. In general, when you accept the loan agreement you are agreeing to repay the loan plus fees. Failure to pay the loan on time usually results in additional penalty fees.

Credit Score

Payday loan companies do not report to the three national bureaus: Equifax, TransUnion and Experian. So applying for a payday loan will not impact your credit score. On the otherhand, many collection agengies do report to the three major bureaus so failure to repay your loan may adversely impact your credit score.

Loan Renewal

Loan renewal policies depend on your specific lender and are disclosed in your loan agreement. In some cases loan renewals may be automatic which means you will incur additional fees.
